GEODES

Click objects erupting from the volcano to crack them open. Geodes contain valuable gems, while rocks just break apart. Fireballs explode and break all geodes on screen at once. The game ends when 20 geodes escape the screen.

🌋 Geodes — Dark spheres with silver specks. Click to crack open and release gems.
🪨 Rocks — Gray stones. Click to break into pieces (no gems, no penalty for missing).
🔥 Fireballs — Rare glowing orbs. Click to explode and turn all geodes into diamonds!
🚀 Rockets — Earn by collecting crystals. Right-click to launch. Explodes on impact! Lead your target!
